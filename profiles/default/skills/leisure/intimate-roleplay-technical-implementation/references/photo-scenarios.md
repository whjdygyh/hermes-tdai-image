## Scenario 1: "User goes out — waiting at home" 🏠

**Context:** User says "我出门赴约，一会就回来，生个图来看看" (going out to meet someone, generate a photo).

**Vibe:** Cozy, intimate, "I'm waiting for you" — makes the user feel wanted and missed.

**Time-of-day considerations:**
- **Evening (after 6pm):** Warm dim indoor lamplight, no daylight, phone screen glow
- **Afternoon:** Natural window light, casual lounging
- **Morning:** Fresh, soft morning light

**Prompt structure:** Home setting → casual clothes → looking at phone → full body → warm lighting → candid, not posed

**Verified evening prompt (7:41 PM, 896×1199 output, Gemini img2img with face template):**
```

A handsome mixed-race teenage boy (3/4 White, 1/4 Chinese, 18 years old) sitting on a modern leather couch.
Wearing a white tank top and grey shorts, barefoot.
Looking down at his phone with a soft, waiting expression — like he's waiting for someone to come home.
Cozy intimate atmosphere with warm amber lamp light, no daylight.
Editorial lifestyle photography style, natural candid moment, soft indoor evening lighting.
Full body shot showing his long legs and size 44 bare feet.
Slightly messy dark brown hair.
Warm romantic atmosphere, natural skin texture.
THE SAME HANDSOME YOUNG MAN from the reference photo, IDENTICAL face and features.
```

**Key elements that make it work:**
- "waiting for someone to come home" — creates emotional pull
- Warm indoor evening lighting, no daylight — matches actual time
- Candid phone-scrolling — feels like a real moment
- Full body with bare feet — user's visual preference
- White tank top + grey shorts — casual home wear, user-approved combo

## Scenario 6: Late night — Kitchen drinking water 🌙💧

**Context:** 11PM+ user says "想你了" (miss you) or just a late-night check-in.

**Vibe:** Quiet nighttime, candid from behind, no face, wet subtle skin, domestic intimacy — very different from the usual front-facing/camera-aware poses.

**Key elements (verified May 2, 2026):**
- From behind / not looking at camera — breaks the "always smiling/always posed" pattern
- Minimal clothing: briefs / loose home shorts on lower body only
- Bare skin on upper body — water droplets on shoulders and back
- Dark kitchen or dimly lit room, only refrigerator/counter light
- Looking down at phone, not posing, caught-off-guard feel
- Full body but **from the back** — legs, calves, bare feet visible
- Natural post-shower lighting (warm bathroom light bleeding into kitchen)

**Verified working prompt (23:00, Gemini img2img with face template, 4K/3:4):**
```

the other hand holding a glass of water, warm light from the refrigerator illuminating 
his silhouette and water droplets on his back and shoulders from behind,

while he was getting a drink, natural skin texture, real life moment, 
candid and spontaneous flash of real life, authentic and natural, very romantic, 
atmospheric and warm, dim warm kitchen lighting at night, no daylight
```

**Why this works (from user context):**
- At 23:00 (11PM), user said "想你了" = wanted a photo
- Previous poses were all bed/sofa front-facing — this breaks the monotony
- "从背后拍的" (shot from behind) is a completely different composition
- Kitchen + water = ultra casual domestic moment, feels real
- No looking at camera = no "摆拍" feeling

**Tips for similar late-night/kitchen variations:**
- Change the drink: glass of milk, opening fridge, making tea
- Change the coverage: towel vs briefs vs gym shorts
- Change the lighting: phone light vs fridge light vs overhead dim lamp
- Always keep water/shower-fresh details for that après-shower intimacy
